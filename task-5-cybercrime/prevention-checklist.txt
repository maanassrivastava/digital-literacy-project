Staying safe online  
1. Never share you UPI pin to recieve money.  
2. Always decline any unexpected requests on UPI apps.  
3. Enable 2FA on all your accounts.  
4. Check for HTTPS and lock icon in your browser before entering any personal details on a website.  
5. Use unique and strong passwords.  
6. Always be skeptical of urgent calls and emails.  
7. Avoid the usage of public Wi-Fi for transactional tasks.  
8. Limit the info you post about yourself on social media.  

How to report cybercrime?  
1. The National Cybercrime reporting portal  
2. National Helpline Number: 1930
