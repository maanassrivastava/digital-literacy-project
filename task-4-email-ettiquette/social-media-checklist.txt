Social Media Checklist  

The Do's:  
1. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated.  
2. Use your college email address for professional sign-ups and academic platform.  
3. Think before you post  
4. Enable 2FA for all accounts  
5. Verify information from multiple sources before sharing.  

The Dont's:  
1. Don't use informal greetings or slangs when in a professional environment.  
2. Don't overshare personal details.  
3. Don't post confidential materials.  
4. Don't engage in cyberbullying.  
5. Don't click on suspicious links.
